To vote, please submit your Elector ID and Password.

As a Calgary Co-op member-owner, you can vote in the annual Director Election.

To view the ballot and vote online, please fill out the fields below before 11:59 MDT on March 13, 2024.

Please Note:

  • Ballots can only be cast by the person(s) whose name appears on the Calgary Co-op membership.
  • If you have a joint membership, please enter your membership number as follows
    • a. Primary member: enter your full membership number (e.g., 123456)
    • b. Secondary member: enter your full membership number, followed by the letter A ( (e.g., 123456A)
  • Members can only choose to vote online OR by paper ballot. In the instance a member votes online and by paper ballot, the online vote will be the only one counted. If you require assistance, please contact:
    memberengagement@calgarycoop.com(opens in a new window) or call 1-833-310-0095
